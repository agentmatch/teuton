MTA - Mineral, Placer and Coal Tenure Spatial View The mineral, placer and coal spatial information is made available for public viewing and download. This is the spatial view used by Mineral Titles Online (MTO) on the mineral, placer and coal viewers. The spatial view combines the polygon information with attribute information for each title.
